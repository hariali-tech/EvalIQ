Frequently Asked Questions
Can EvalIQ read handwritten answer sheets?
Yes — EvalIQ uses TrOCR, a Microsoft AI model, to read handwriting from scanned PDFs or photos. It works on most exam answer sheets.
Is my student data safe?
Yes — EvalIQ runs entirely on your institution's own server. No student data is ever sent to an external cloud service.
Can I override an AI grade?
Yes — every AI grade can be overridden. Just enter a new score and provide a reason. The change is recorded in the audit trail automatically.
What is Bloom's Taxonomy and why does it appear?
EvalIQ classifies each answer into one of 6 thinking levels — from Remember (basic recall) to Create (original thinking). This tells students not just their score but how deeply they understood, and what to do to improve.
Do I need to train the AI or set anything technical up?
No. The AI models are pre-installed and ready. All you need to do is create a class, add a rubric, and upload answer scripts.
